What You’ll Learn

From foundations to hands-on installations: design, install, configure and run solar-inverter systems.

Module 1: Intro to Solar Energy

Types of systems, solar radiation basics and how panels generate electricity.

Module 2: Solar System Design

Load calculations, panel/inverter/battery sizing and layout planning.

Module 3: Inverter Installation

Rack, wire, configure and commission inverters for resilient systems.

Module 4: Battery Management

BMS basics, battery configurations, safety and longevity strategies.

Module 5: Safety Standards

Codes, earthing, PPE, and professional installation best practices.

Module 6: Business Setup

Quoting, client acquisition, project management and post-sales support.

Meet Victor Alamu — Your Host

Victor Alamu — Electrical & Electronic Engineer • Renewable Energy Specialist

Hi! I’m Victor Alamu, founder of VOAL Technology - a solar servicing and technological company and also, a co-fouder of Tubim Energy Ltd, where we design scalable energy products. With over 7 years of experience in the field. I started with electrical installations for homes and businesses and later specialized in solar-inverter systems.

I led a mini-grid project for the Adiyan rural community (Ogun State) and have overseen numerous residential and commercial solar projects. I’ve seen how reliable energy transforms productivity, livelihoods, and communities — and I’m committed to making that change accessible.

That’s why I’m offering this free training: I’m pouring in years of learning, real-world experience, and mentorship so you can gain the skills to start earning and empower your community.
